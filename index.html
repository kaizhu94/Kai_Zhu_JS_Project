Players, ready to fight for your favorite Meme? Follow the instruction below:

  1. Player 1 can use keys W, A, S, D to move the puzzle

  2. Player 2 can use keys Up, Left, Down, Right to move the puzzle

  3. Both players can also move the puzzle by clicking on images around the empty spot.

  4. Press Space Bar or use Pause Button on the left for music
